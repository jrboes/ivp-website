## Techniques I Use

**Psychosynthesis**  
Working with our sub-Personalities to see and understand our beliefs through emotions. The sub-personalities reside in the sub-conscious and in a sense, acting out our beliefs. By examining them, we bring our beliefs into the light for examination. From there, we choose if we want to keep the belief or release it.

**Archetypes**  
Archetypes are universal patterns in the collective unconscious. We are all a part of forming and maintaining these patterns. Knowing the archetypes that work strongest in us gives us insights into where our power lies. We have strengths we may not be aware of that can open up worlds of opportunity and we can discover activities that sap our energy because they are not a part of who we really are.

Knowing the archetypes that work strongest in us gives us insights into where our power lies. We have strengths we are not aware of. Strengths when used can open up worlds of opportunity. 

**Dream Analysis**  
Dream analysis brings out repressed aspects of our unconscious personality. We can recognize different parts of our personality when we say we are of two minds or when we say part of me thinks this but another part of me thinks that. These are parts of ourselves we normally try to ignore. Dream work brings them into a sharper focus letting us know we need to pay attention to these and not get rid of but heal.

**Spiritual Awareness**  
Spiritual awareness is recognizing our connection to the Divine, eternal Self or the interconnected nature of all things. It involves a deeper awareness of our true nature, purpose and relationship within the universe.

Mindfulness helps us become more aware of this interconnectedness. It makes us aware of how we perceive ourselves, others and the world. As we examine, we come to honor our worthiness,  love, our specialness and how we fit into the all.
