## Trauma Recovery:
My Trauma Recovery Coaching focuses on working with childhood trauma that created a strong inner critic and a shameful sense of self. This type of trauma can result in disconnection in relationships; relationships we have with ourselves and with others. It may be we either we lash out at others or turn within and punish ourselves when we feel threatened which can result in damaged relationships and sometimes depression. 

My objective is to help my clients open up to a more authentic and fulfilling life through awareness and self-compassion. 

My premise of understanding is based on the fact that we are all worthy and loving at our core and we all deserve to be loved and in supportive relationships.

My process is to educate my clients on how the body responds to ongoing traumatic events and how those coping mechanisms keep us stuck in the past when we were dependent on our caregivers for love and attention but did not get what we needed. Trauma is embodied in us and needs to be addressed from the body as well as the mind. 

We do not need to be fixed. We need to embrace the different parts of ourselves with understanding and self-compassion to be able to live our lives more fully.

## Techniques I Use

**Psychosynthesis**  
Working with our sub-Personalities (our Parts) to see and understand our beliefs created through emotions.
The sub-personalities reside in the sub-conscious and in a sense, acting out our beliefs. 
By examining them, we bring our beliefs into the light for examination and look at the conflicting beliefs 
held by different parts. Some of these beliefs are created in childhood and no longer serve who we are
 today. In becoming aware of these thoughts we hold, we give ourselves the space to choose what
 decision to make for any situation. 

Archetypes and Dream Analysis
My work also includes identifying Archetypes and doing Dream Analysis. Archetypes are universal patterns in the collective unconscious. 
We are all a part of forming and maintaining these patterns and they are played out in our lives.
Knowing the archetypes that work strongest in us gives us insights into where our power lies. 

Dream analysis brings out repressed aspects of our unconscious personality. 
It bring our parts into a sharper focus letting us know where we might pay more attention
and increase awareness.

Spiritual Awareness
Spiritual awareness is recognizing our connection to the Divine, eternal Self or the interconnected nature
 of all things. It involves a deeper awareness of our true nature, purpose and relationship within the 
universe.
Mindfulness helps us become more aware of this interconnectedness. It makes us aware of how we 
perceive ourselves, others and the world. As we examine, we come to honor our worthiness, love,
 our specialness and how we fit into the all.
